Our Mission

To make mindfulness and happiness accessible to everyone

DrukMind is inspired by the Kingdom of Bhutan's profound philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH). We believe that true well-being comes from a balanced development of mind, body, and spirit.

Our app brings together ancient Bhutanese wisdom and modern mindfulness practices to help you cultivate peace, clarity, and happiness in your daily life.

Our Story

Born from the Land of the Thunder Dragon

DrukMind was conceived during a journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Bhutan. We were inspired by the country's commitment to happiness and well-being over material growth.

Our team of mindfulness practitioners, developers, and designers came together to create an app that shares Bhutan's unique approach to life with the world.

We are committed to creating a positive impact by helping individuals and communities thrive through mindfulness.
